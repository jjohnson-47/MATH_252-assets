Learning Objectives

  • Define and calculate antiderivatives using basic integration rules
  • Explain the relationship between derivatives and indefinite integrals
  • Evaluate definite integrals using the Fundamental Theorem of Calculus
  • Understand Riemann sums and their relationship to definite integrals
  • Apply geometric interpretation to calculate areas under curves

This Week's Topics

Section 5.1 - Antiderivatives and Indefinite Integration

Introduction to antiderivatives, basic integration rules, and the relationship between derivatives and indefinite integrals. We'll explore how to reverse the differentiation process.

Section 5.2 - The Definite Integral

Understanding definite integrals, their properties, and their geometric interpretation as signed area. We'll see how integrals quantify accumulation.

Section 5.3 - Riemann Sums

Exploring how to approximate areas using Riemann sums and understanding the limit process that leads to the definite integral.
