Course Overview - MATH A252 Calculus 2

Asynchronous Course Format

  • No Required Live Meetings: This class is primarily asynchronous, meaning you will not need to log in for scheduled group meetings.
  • Optional Zoom Sessions: Occasional live Zoom help or Q&A sessions will be scheduled. A list of these will be shared once the course begins.
  • Where to Participate: Blackboard, Discussion Boards, Edfinity (online homework), and email are the main communication tools.

Expectations & Due Dates

  • Stay Current: Familiarize yourself with all assignment deadlines and exam dates. Weekly assignments follow this pattern:
    • Reading Quizzes due Mondays by 11:59 PM
    • BB Assignments and Edfinity Homework due Fridays by 11:59 PM
    • Written Homework due alternate Fridays by 11:59 PM when assigned
  • Late Work Policy: Late submissions are discouraged and rarely accepted; contact your instructor in unusual circumstances.
