Email Policy and Account Management

Important Account Management: Students are strongly suggested to keep their @alaska.edu accounts separate from personal and school district accounts. These systems have different expectations and purposes. Your university accounts are connected to several services (Google, Microsoft) and should be used exclusively for university-related communication.

Official Email Account

  • UA Google Account: All course emails will be sent to your UAA/KPC Google account.
  • Account Setup: Adjust your profile, name, and settings in UA Online.
  • Check Often: Log in regularly or set up forwarding if you prefer another email service.
  • Privacy Notice: Grade-related information will not be sent to non-UA addresses.

Email Etiquette

Professional Communication: All email communication should maintain a professional tone and format.
  • Preferred Method: Send emails from Blackboard if possible.
  • Subject Line: Include the course name for clarity (e.g., "MATH A252 - Question about Assignment 1").
  • Professional Tone: Use correct spelling, grammar, and punctuation; sign with your full name.

Required Setup Task

Send an Introduction Email
  • Subject: "Configuration"
  • Body: Briefly describe your computer setup (hardware & software), confirm it meets course requirements, and sign with your full name.
Optional Email Forwarding: If you choose not to use your UA Google account directly:
  • Follow the instructions at UA Google Apps to set up email forwarding
  • Test the forwarding setup to ensure it works properly
  • Note that you're still responsible for all course communications
