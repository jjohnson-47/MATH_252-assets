Technology Requirements

Hardware Requirements

  • Computer: A newer computer (PC or Mac) with updated operating systems (Windows 10+ or Mac OS 10.9+) is recommended. Linux or Chrome OS can work but may require extra configuration.
  • Internet: A reliable high-speed internet connection is essential for streaming course videos and completing online tasks.
  • Headset/Mic: A headset with a microphone is required for online office hours or live Zoom sessions.

Required Software & Tools

  • Web Browsers: Install at least two browsers (e.g., Google Chrome, Firefox) in case one performs better for particular tasks or websites.
  • Google Docs: Required for document creation and editing.
  • PDF Reader: Adobe Reader (recommended) or another lightweight PDF viewer (Sumatra PDF).
  • Microsoft Office 365: Available free to all UAA/KPC students (online and desktop versions).
  • Calculators: Access to the following online calculators is required:
    • Desmos Scientific Calculator (desmos.com/scientific)
    • Desmos Graphing Calculator (desmos.com/calculator)
    • GeoGebra Calculators and Tools
